About Gamma Epsilon

Pi Kappa Alpha at Utah State University

Our Brotherhood

The Gamma Epsilon Chapter of Pi Kappa Alpha at Utah State University is home to a brotherhood built on the pillars of Scholarship, Leadership, Athletics, and Gentlemanly Conduct. We hold ourselves to the highest standard on campus and in our community.

Our brothers are scholars, athletes, leaders, and lifelong friends. From Aggie athletics to community service, Gamma Epsilon is active in every corner of Utah State campus life — in the classroom, on the field, in student government, and in service to Cache Valley.

We carry the legacy of Pi Kappa Alpha — founded on March 1, 1868 at the University of Virginia — and honor that tradition every single day in Logan, Utah. As members of one of North America's largest and most respected fraternities, Gamma Epsilon brothers join a network of over 310,000 lifetime initiates who have gone on to lead in business, government, athletics, and the arts.

Brotherhood at Gamma Epsilon isn't just a word — it's a commitment. We push each other to be better, hold each other accountable, and celebrate each other's wins. When you join PIKE, you don't just gain a fraternity. You gain a family.
