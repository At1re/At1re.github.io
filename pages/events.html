Spring 2025 Calendar

Mar12025
Founders Day Celebration
Annual Tradition · Chapter-Wide · Formal Dinner
On March 1st, 1868, Pi Kappa Alpha was founded in Room 47 of West Range at the University of Virginia. Every year we honor that legacy with a formal dinner, brotherhood ceremony, and a recommitment to the values that define our fraternity. This is our most cherished tradition — a night that belongs to every Pike, past and present.
Mar152025
Spring Rush Kickoff
Recruitment · Open to All Men · Chapter House
Come meet the brothers of Gamma Epsilon. Whether you're a freshman finding your footing or an upperclassman ready to make a move — our doors are open. No pressure, no obligation. Just brotherhood, food, and a chance to see what PIKE is really about.
Mar222025
Community Service Day
Service · Cache Valley · All Brothers Required
Brothers volunteer across Logan and Cache Valley in our quarterly chapter-wide service day. We believe in giving back to the community that gives so much to us. Projects include park cleanups, food bank volunteering, and neighborhood service projects.
Apr52025
Philanthropy Week
Campus-Wide · Multiple Days · Greek Competition
Our signature week of philanthropy brings together the entire USU Greek community for competitions, fundraisers, and community events — all benefiting our chapter's charity partner. Past years have featured wiffle ball tournaments, obstacle courses, and auctions. This is one of the most fun weeks of the year.

Rush Round 2 — Brotherhood Night
Recruitment · Invite Only
The second round of Spring Rush. Rushees who attended the kickoff get an invite to a smaller, more personal brotherhood night. A chance to connect with individual brothers and get a real sense of what daily life in PIKE looks like.

USU Intramural Championships
Athletics · Spring Playoffs · USU Campus
PIKE competes for intramural titles across multiple sports this spring. Brotherhood is forged on the field. Come cheer on your brothers as we compete for another championship banner. All friends and family are welcome.

End of Year Banquet
Formal Event · Black Tie · Brothers & Guests
A formal dinner celebrating the accomplishments of our chapter over the past academic year. Awards are presented, seniors are honored, and the full brotherhood comes together one last time before summer. Black tie, brother's choice of guest. One of the best nights of the year.

Brotherhood · Chapter-Wide · Closed Ceremony
We welcome our newest class of brothers into the Gamma Epsilon Chapter through Pi Kappa Alpha's sacred initiation ceremony. A milestone that marks the beginning of a lifetime of brotherhood. Congratulations to the incoming class.
