Our Commitment to Service

Giving back is not an obligation at Gamma Epsilon — it's a core part of who we are. Pi Kappa Alpha's founding values call us to be not just leaders within our organization, but contributors to the communities we're a part of. In Logan and across Cache Valley, our brothers show up.

Every semester we organize chapter-wide service days, contribute to campus philanthropy events, and individually volunteer with local organizations. Our annual Philanthropy Week is one of the biggest events in USU Greek life — bringing together fraternities and sororities for friendly competition while raising money for causes that matter.

We're proud of what we've built, and we're always working to do more. Philanthropy is the most visible expression of who we want to be outside our own walls.

Philanthropy Week

Every spring, Gamma Epsilon hosts Philanthropy Week — a multi-day campus event that brings together the entire USU Greek community for competitions, fundraisers, and service. It's one of the most anticipated weeks of the year, and one of our proudest accomplishments as a chapter.

Past Philanthropy Weeks have featured wiffle ball tournaments, flag football games, obstacle courses, auctions, and more. All proceeds benefit our designated charity partner for the year. Greek organizations compete for points, but the real winner is always the cause.
