Mar12025
Founders Day Celebration
Annual Tradition · Chapter-Wide · Formal Dinner
On March 1st, 1868, Pi Kappa Alpha was founded in Room 47 of West Range at the University of Virginia. Every year we honor that legacy with a formal dinner, brotherhood ceremony, and a recommitment to the values that define our fraternity. This is our most sacred tradition — a night that belongs to every Pike, past and present.

Spring Rush Kickoff
Recruitment · Open to All Men at USU
Come meet the brothers of Gamma Epsilon. Whether you're a freshman finding your footing or an upperclassman ready for something more — our doors are open. No pressure, no obligation. Just brotherhood, good food, and a real look at what PIKE is about.

Service · Cache Valley · All Brothers
Brothers volunteer across Logan and Cache Valley in our quarterly service day. Projects include park cleanups, food bank volunteering, and neighborhood service. We believe in giving back to the community that gives so much to us.
